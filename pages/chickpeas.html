Ingredients

Chickpeas
Toasted Sesame Oil
1 Lime of Half a Lemon
Salt

Instructions

  1. Drain chickpeas
  2. Salt chickpeas
  3. Add chickpeas to air fryer: air fry for 11-12 minutes at 200C
  4. Take out chickpeas into bowl or mug
  5. Squeeze the lemon or lime over it and add a drizzle of sesame oil
